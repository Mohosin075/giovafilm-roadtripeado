Greetings, the prizes can be one of two things:
1. A PDF itinerary
2. Free activation of any map you want.
How will users earn the prizes?.
Users will accumulate points by writing reviews and uploading photos in the "Reviews" section. When
a user accumulates a certain number of points, they unlock the prize.
PDF Itinerary Prize: If it's the PDF itinerary, a brief description of the itinerary and a "download"
button may appear.
Free Map Prize: If the map has been won, a button to select a free map may appear. Once a user
selects their preferred map, they will not be able to select another.
Everything will be based on points and levels, but the prizes may change and more may be added.
